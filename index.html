Crystals Collector

Here are the rules:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystal below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a number to your current score. Each crystal has a different value that is hidden from you until you click on it.
You win by matching your total score to the random number. You lose by your score exceeding the random number.
Good luck!
